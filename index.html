Team Paper Prototype: Helping the Community

Our app starts by explaining how to log in or create an account in order to use it. When you sign up, you'll be asked to enter certain details, etc. Following that, the application will prompt the user to indicate their search terms and desired level of proximity to the location. After that, the user will be taken to the options they have chosen. our vehicles, housing, education, and job listings. A few open jobs will be visible to the user if they choose the job listings tab. When a user applies for a job, they submit an application. A few days later, the user receives a notification in the app indicating whether or not their application was accepted. The user will see available vehicles if they choose the vehicles tab. The user will see housing options and the application to apply for it if they choose housing. The user will see community and technical colleges if they choose the education option.
